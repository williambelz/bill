Community Advocate

Hi, I'm Billy. For over 20 years I have been dedicated to helping people with disabilities succeed. My career began as a job coach assisting individuals in finding their first opportunities in the workplace.

Professional Journey

Since those early days, I've worked as a case manager supporting people with intellectual and developmental disabilities (IDD) and autism spectrum disorder (ASD). I then served as a vocational counselor, guiding clients toward meaningful employment. Along the way, I also explored web design to make online resources more accessible and user friendly.

Today

I now focus on artificial intelligence solutions that improve accessibility and vocational services. This site shares what I've learned so far and offers tools for professionals, clients and community members.

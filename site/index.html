Empowering Everyone: Accessibility in Vocational Rehabilitation

This site provides guidance on accessibility features for vocational rehabilitation services. Explore how inclusive design opens doors for all users.

What is Accessibility?

Accessibility ensures that people with disabilities can use digital tools and services without barriers. It includes everything from screen reader compatibility to keyboard navigation.

Role of Vocational Rehabilitation

Vocational rehabilitation programs help individuals with disabilities gain employment and independence through training, accommodations and assistive technology.

Accessibility Statement

We are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for individuals with disabilities. We continuously improve the user experience for everyone and apply the relevant accessibility standards.

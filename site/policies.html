Legal Requirements

Digital services in vocational rehabilitation must comply with accessibility laws such as Section 508 in the United States. These regulations mandate that electronic and information technology be accessible to people with disabilities.

Best Practices

Following the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) ensures that websites and applications meet widely accepted accessibility standards. Routine audits and user testing help maintain compliance.

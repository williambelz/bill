Recent Findings

As of 2025-06-05, research in digital accessibility highlights:

  • WebAIM Million 2024 analyzed one million home pages and found that 96.3% had WCAG 2 failures. The top issues include low contrast text, missing alternative text and empty links. [Source: WebAIM]
  • The 2024 State of Accessibility Report (Fable) indicates that organizations investing in inclusive design see increased user satisfaction and reduced legal risk. [Source: Fable]
  • WCAG 2.2 was finalized in October 2023, introducing new success criteria to improve accessibility for low-vision and cognitive disability users. [Source: W3C]

This summary compiles public findings available as of 2025-06-05.

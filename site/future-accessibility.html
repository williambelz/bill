AI-Driven Assistive Technology

Machine learning enhances screen readers, captioning and image descriptions, delivering richer context for blind and low-vision users.

Real-Time Captioning & Sign Language

Improvements in speech recognition and sign-language avatars promise live communication support for people who are deaf or hard of hearing.

Adaptive Interfaces

Interfaces will automatically adjust for different abilities, offering personalized layouts and voice-driven controls for those with limited mobility.

Immersive Technologies

Augmented and virtual reality can deliver inclusive training with tactile feedback, supporting amputees and others using prosthetics.

Emerging Regulations

Anticipated updates to the ADA and Section 508 will address accessibility challenges in new forms of digital interaction.

History of VR in the U.S.

Vocational rehabilitation programs began in the early 20th century to assist injured workers and veterans. Today they support people with disabilities in achieving employment and independence.

Accessibility Laws

Key laws such as the ADA, the Rehabilitation Act (Sections 504 and 508) and the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act shape accessibility requirements.

Goals of VR Programs

VR aims to provide job training, accommodations and assistive technology so clients can thrive in the workforce.

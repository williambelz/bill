Screen Readers and Assistive Technology

Digital platforms must integrate with screen readers and other assistive technology used by people in vocational rehabilitation. Proper labeling and ARIA roles ensure compatibility.

Captioning and Transcripts

Videos should include captions and transcripts to assist users with hearing impairments. This is critical for training materials and webinars.
